# Handle  0xRajeev   # Vulnerability details  ## Impact  Most contracts use initialize() functions instead of constructor given the delegatecall proxy pattern. While most of them emit an event in the critical initialize() functions to record the init parameters for off-chain monitoring and transparency reasons, Ticket.sol nor its base class ControlledToken.sol emit such an event in their initialize() functions.
